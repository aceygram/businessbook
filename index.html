About the Book

The Real Reason Small Businesses Fail

Most business books talk about success. This one starts with failure — and that's exactly what makes it different. Chisom Ifeanacho Okeke built businesses generating hundreds of millions in turnover, but not before losing money, making expensive mistakes, and navigating without a mentor.

This book is his unfiltered account of every hard lesson — how mixing personal and business finances nearly broke him, why operating without a mentor cost him years, and how vision and structure were the missing keys that unlocked real growth.

"Financial literacy is not optional for business success — it's essential. Without it, no amount of hard work or talent can prevent chaos."

Meet the Author
Chisom Ifeanacho Okeke
Entrepreneur · Business Builder · Founder, Crystal Diagnostics Centre

Chisom Ifeanacho Okeke is the entrepreneur behind two successful businesses — a Tigernut beverage brand and Crystal Diagnostics Centre, a healthcare facility serving thousands of patients. His journey started with financial confusion, no mentor, and no clear structure. Through hard lessons, deliberate learning, and the relentless application of core principles, he built businesses generating hundreds of millions in turnover. In this book, he shares everything he learned — not to impress, but to equip the next generation of entrepreneurs to go further, faster, and with fewer costly mistakes.

"Failure is not the opposite of success — it is part of it."
